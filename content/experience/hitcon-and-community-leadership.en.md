Beyond my day job, a major part of my work is building the conditions that help the wider cybersecurity ecosystem thrive.

Key roles include:

- Executive Director of the Association of Hackers in Taiwan
- Review Board Member for HITCON Conference
- Review Board Member for HITCON Training
- General Coordinator of HITCON CISO Summit 2023, 2024, 2025, and 2026
- Deputy General Coordinator of HITCON 2020
- General Coordinator of HITCON 2021 and 2022

This work matters because it connects research, practice, leadership, and public conversation. It is also one of the most visually compelling parts of my background for a personal site.
