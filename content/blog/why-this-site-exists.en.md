I wanted this site to do more than act as a résumé in HTML.

Most of my work sits at the boundary between research, operations, leadership, and community: building product defense programs, investigating emerging threats, teaching practitioners, supporting public-sector resilience, and helping convene serious security conversations across organizations.

This site is where I am starting to collect that work in a form that is easier to browse, reference, and build on over time.
