Across conferences, trainings, and workshops, I have repeatedly focused on operational technology, critical infrastructure, and the engineering realities of cyber defense where availability and safety matter as much as confidentiality.

Topics in this area include:

- ICS and OT threat research
- resilience in critical environments
- product and enterprise defense for industrial systems
- communication between technical teams, leadership, and operational stakeholders

Representative speaking venues include:

- Black Hat USA, Black Hat Europe, and Black Hat MEA
- RSA Conference
- FIRST
- HITB, HITCON, Troopers, SecTor, S4, SINCON, and ROOTCON
- ICS Cyber Security Conference USA and Asia

Representative talk examples include:

- Dissecting the Industrial Communication Protocols for Cybersecurity Risks
- The Sum of All Fears, When ICS/SCADA are Compromised
- Public and Private, Common Flaws in ICS Network Protocols
- Data Breaches Related to Critical Infrastructure
