This track represents the work I care most about operationally: turning emerging threat research into something defenders and product teams can actually use.

Representative themes:

- malware analysis and adversary tradecraft
- threat intelligence and hunting
- building and scaling detection capabilities
- PSIRT, product security response, and product hardening
- converting research into practical tools, processes, and training

Common venues for this work include:

- Black Hat, RSA Conference, CODE BLUE, FIRST, and DEF CON Village
- HITCON, CYBERSEC, NOHAT, Troopers, and ROOTCON
- invited enterprise sessions related to product security, research, and operational defense

This track most clearly reflects the continuity between my earlier research work and my current responsibilities at TXOne: malware analysis, detection logic, threat intelligence, and the translation of research into product defense and incident response.
